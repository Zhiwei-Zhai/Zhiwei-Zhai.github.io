Short Bio

Currently, I am working as a Postdoc in the Cardiovascular Lab at the Amsterdam University Medical Centers, under the supervision of Prof. dr. Ivana Išgum. My current project is on deep generative models to synthesize CT data. On 10th March 2020, I defended my PhD dissertation at the Division of Image Processing, Leiden University Medical Center, supervised by Dr. Berend C. Stoel and Dr. Marius Staring. Before that, I obtained my Master degree in the Harbin Institute of Technology, School of Computer Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Yuanzhi Cheng in July 2014. I received Bachelor degree in Department of Mathematics from Harbin Institute of Technology (weihai) in July 2012. I worked with Prof. Dr. Dejun Fan for undergraduate research.

Research Interests

Medical Image Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Geometric Deep Learning, Pulmonary Vessel Quantification.
